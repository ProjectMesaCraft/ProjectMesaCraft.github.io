Small Update {3/21/2021}

This is a pretty small update, mainly to fix the little things that needed changes (Kind of like a day 1 update). Please read through them thoroughly, as some of the changes can be important to your playstyle and keeping your in-game progress safe.

Added Plugins:

TimeVote:

WeatherVote:

LogPrevention (CombatLogX):

Claiming:

****Mob-griefing for your protected land is enabled by default. To prevent mobs & others using this to their advantage, disable the mob-griefing setting inside your claim chest. This wasn't possible to change before, so be sure to change this if you want to protect yourself from mobs & players. The other settings cannot be changed.

GIF explaining how-to:

Timevote:

****Instead of adding OnePlayer sleep, we decided to add this! It costs $1000 each vote you make that wins.

Commands: /tv day /tv night /tv yes - Vote yes for the outgoing vote /tv no - Vote no for the outgoing vote

Weathervote:

****You can also vote to change the weather! This will also cost $1000 for a vote.

Commands:

/wv sun

/wv rain

/wv yes

/wv no

LogPrevention:

****It seems a lot of you enjoy yourself some PVP, which caught us a little off guard. I guess we should've expected a feisty play style. This will fix any issue with players combat-logging while in PVP or trying to evade death with silly solutions. When somebody logs, they will instantly die. So don't.

Other Changes:

Added a cooldown to using ./tpa & ./home

Added delay to ./tpa & ./home

Iron & Diamond give more time for your claim

Tree-cutting cooldown lowered

Raised price of Iron at spawn
