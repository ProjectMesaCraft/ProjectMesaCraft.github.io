AutoCraft

After building the contraption, all it takes is placing the resources in one chest and watch the process flow. To build, put an itemframe with a craftingtable in it on a dispenser. Place two chests on each side and put the recipe of the item you'd like tocraft in the dispenser. The dispenser should be pointed towards the chest you want it to spit the outcome into.

ChestSort

ChestSort can automatically sort every chest, barrel, etc. after you have used it. This also works to organize your player-inventory. You can use both commands or built-in hotkeys to use this feature. Also, every hotkey can be enabled/disabled through the GUI.

Link to plugin

Commands:

/sort or /chestsort

Toggle automatic chest sorting.

/sort on|off or /chestsort on|off

Enable/disable automatic chest sorting

/sort hotkeys or /chestsort hotkeys

Open the hotkeys GUI to enable/disable hotkeys

/invsort or /isort

Sort Inventory

/invsort hotbar or /isort hotbar

Sort Hotbar

/invsort toggle or /isort toggle

Toggle automatic inventory sorting

/invsort on|off or /isort on|off

Enable/disable automatic inventory sorting


Hotkeys List:

Middle click

Middle click (mousewheel) on ANY inventory slot

Shift click

Shift + left click on any EMPTY inventory slot

Double click

Double click on any EMPTY inventory slot

Shift + right click

Shift + right click on any EMPTY inventory slot

Additional Hotkeys

Left-Click outside inventory

Puts all matching stuff (except hotbar) into the chest, barrel etc.

Double-Left-Click outside inventory

Puts all items (except hotbar) into the chest, barrel etc.

Right-Click outside inventory

Puts matching items from the chest, barrel etc. into your inventory

Double-Right-Click outside inventory

Empties the whole chest, barrel etc. into your inventory
