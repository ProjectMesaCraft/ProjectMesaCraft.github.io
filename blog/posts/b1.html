Update "Bee Innovative" {3/2/2021}

Server Event:

We're finally getting around to doing a server event! It's a race to the finish line, only the best at speedrunning will reach the end. (haha, get it?)

For the event, we will be hosting a race to the end. First to make it and kill the ender dragon wins a $10 nitro gift. Second to make it to the end will receive a $5 Nitro Classic gift. Third place doesn't receive any gift.

This event will be hosted on a separate world, you don't have to worry about losing any items from the main server. The rules for this event are simple, no cheating/hacking and have good sportsmanship. Using common sense would be appreciated as well. PVP is allowed, so ruin others day til your hearts content.

Player Roles:

Are you a hardcore player on the server? Do you waste your entire life on just one game? Well, you can finally get something in return! It might encourage being more lazy though.

With the playtime feature we've had running, you now obtain special roles for the more you play! Here is the list of roles achievable:

default - Newcomer

1 hour - Commoner

1 day - Traveler

1 week - Adventurer

1 month - Nu-Lyfer (AKA god of anime)
