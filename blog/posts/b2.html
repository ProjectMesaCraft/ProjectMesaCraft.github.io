Playing Catchup {3/16/2021}

General Changes:

Combat Logging:

Nobody likes limitations, but nobody also likes annoying combat loggers. We've implemented another system we think will not cause major issues, but if any do come along please make a bug report so we can help out and remove the issue quickly.

Griefing/Trolling:

We're adding a change to this rule and will be updated in #rules soon. If somebodies claim is not protected/no longer protected, griefing the builds in said claim is not allowed. Breaking blocks in claims were allowed beforehand, it's not anymore. You can still take others items though.

Plugins added for the Minecraft Server:

    Created a OnePlayerSleep plugin (Replacement for TimeVote & WeatherVote)

    EpicFurnaces

    UltimateFishing

    YouTrade

EpicFurnaces:

Furnaces will be like your best friend, if you already have one that is. If you left-click your furnace, there will be a new GUI. Now, you can upgrade your furnaces to optimize the cooking speed and the amount you get from smelting. But it comes with a price! Be careful of having them overheat, or making your wallet empty. This feature also effects brewing stands.

⚙ Note: Any furnaces currently in the server will either need to be thrown away and replaced or just placed onto the ground again.

UltimateFishing:

Are you bored? Well, this won't help you... but you can earn some side cash! Buy your self some bait, catch a fish and sell em!

Commands: /uf sell /uf baitshop

YouTrade:

Simple. Just a safe way to communicate with people. Scary, I know.

Commands: /trade {player} /trade accept /trade ignore (This ignores all trades)

⚙ Note: You can also Shift right-click a player to initiate a trade.

PlayerVaults AKA Backpacks:

You want a virtual backpack? Say no more! Get one now, as we might make them difficult to "find" in the future!

Commands:

/bp (This can be in any way: bp1, bp 1, bp etc.)

⚙ Note: It will cost $2500 per backpack, 3 max.
